CREATIVE MOROCCAN KITCHEN (CUISINE) 2023

Moroccan cuisine features a wide range of ingredients, including Mediterranean fruits, vegetables, beef, goat, mutton, lamb, chicken, and seafood. Common flavorings include lemon pickle, argan oil. preserved butter (smen), and olive oil. Staple grains include rice and wheat, used in bread and couscous. Spices like cinnamon, cumin, turmeric, and saffron are used extensively, with 27 spices combined to create the Moroccan spice mixture ras el hanout.
